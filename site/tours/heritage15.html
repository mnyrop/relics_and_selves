Colonial beginnings





Catálogo razonado, 'Esposicion del Coloniaje'

The re-appraisal of the colonial past had already begun in Chile in the 1870s, the monumental and enormously succesful 'Esposicion del Coloniaje' (organised by the historian and mayor of Santiago, Benjamín Vicuña Mackenna) being probably the first mass-cultural event in Chilean history – an interesting detail in an era where great exhibitions and fairs would usually be organised around themes of progress and future development.
