The royal body





Genaro Pérez, Retrato del General Roca


Yet republican dignataries also resorted to painterly representation, as you can see on this portrait of Argentina's strongman of the late nineteenth century, General Julio Argentino Roca, president from 1880 to 1886 and then again from 1898 to 1904. This painting by Genaro Pérez depicts him during his first presidency, won largely on account of the so-called 'Desert Campaign' of 1879, which occupied large parts of territory until then inhabited by indigenous groups.
