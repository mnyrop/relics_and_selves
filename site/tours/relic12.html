Idols of alterity





Marc Ferrez, Exposição antropológica: artefactos e aspectos da vida indigena (instrumentos musicais)


On the journey from their place of encounter in the Amazon to the remote National Museum of Rio de Janeiro, then, the relics dug up from indigenous cemeteries and other ritual sites, suffered a complex process of dis- and re-appropriation, turning them into fragments of a national fiction of identity and origin that was at the same time excluding their makers from the national community. A spectacular form of re-assemblage of these fragments into a monumental display of Brazilian national –and scientific– identity was the Anthropological Exhibition Netto organised on the Museum's premises in 1882, the first of its kind on the entire American continent.
