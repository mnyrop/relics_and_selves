Bourgeois habitus





Mitín de la Unión Cívica Radical

However, the massification of the city and the rising number of immigrants arriving from overseas, with the subsequent emergence of petty bourgeoisies who would imitate the lifestyle and attire of their wealthier peers, also entailed an increasingly complex system of social distinction and an ability to negotiate contradictory cultural references at one of the same time, as 'native' elements were now used to indicate social status (yet still needed to be counterbalanced by a literacy in European culture so as not to be confounded with the native popular class).
