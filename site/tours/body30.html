The body at war





Julio Fernández Villanueva, Combate de San Lorenzo

War paintings of the struggles of independence or the wars of Paraguay and the Pacific, such as Julio Fernández Villanueva´s 'Battle of San Lorenzo', painted on commission from the Argentine National Historical Museum, forged dramatic representations of the nation as a collective male body at war, pre-cinematic mass scenes that could be displayed side to side with individual portraits of the nation´s war heroes (San Martín, O´Higgins, Prat, Osorio, etc.)
