The monumental city





Marc Ferrez, Viaduto de Silvestre da Estrada de Ferro do Corcovado, 1883

Instead, Brazilian photographers such as Marc Ferrez would focus on the juxtaposition of the tropical landscape and the feats of civic engineering, in order to generate a particular kind of urban sublime – as on this picture of the Silvestre Viaduct on the Corcovado tramway line, the country´s first 'tourist railway', inaugurated in 1882.
