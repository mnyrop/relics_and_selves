Heritage and folklore





Claude Gay, Trajes de la gente

Late colonial and early independence forms of social typification, such as 'casta painting' and costumbristic illustrations in travel books such as Gay's 'Album de Chile' (1854), then, made a reappearance towards the end of the century as a token of folkloristic collectionism and modern nostalgia for a past that suddenly seemed attractive for its slow pace and overseeable social order.
