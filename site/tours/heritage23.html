Indigenous legacies





Horacio Lara, Lautaro convocando al pueblo araucano a la guerra

Chilean historians continued to refer to the Araucanians' fierce resistance against the Spanish invaders as an allegorical foreboding of the nation belligerent nature, even at a time when the descendants of legendary indigenous chiefs such as Lautaro and Caupolican were being violently hostigated and expulsed from their territories. This picture, which is typical in its archaising depiction of Lautaro as a classical hero, is from historian and novelist Horacio Lara's Crónica de la Araucania, first published in 1889 in the newspaper El Progreso.
