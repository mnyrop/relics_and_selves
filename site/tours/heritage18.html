Vitor Meireles de Lima, Primeira missa no Brasil

Twenty years earlier, Meireles had completed what would remain one of his most influential works, and a cornerstone of a kind of historical painting heavily influenced by French academicism yet also often based on a thorough command of historical and ethnographic writing on Brazil.
