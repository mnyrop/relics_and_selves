Having clicked on the 'Search' button, you will enter the virtual showroom your query has generated. It features the titles and small-size reproductions of the images selected, as well as a brief description and the decade of their original production. You can now look in more detail at the exhibits themselves, by clicking on the small-scale images on the right, or study their labels, by clicking on the titles on the left. New windows will then open separately, which you can close in order to return to the showroom.
